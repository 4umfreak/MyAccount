Total gross sales is the total amount of travel services sold by a travel wholesaler or accommodation provider during the most recent fiscal year in BC. These services are the transaction value, or the cost of the sales to consumers, excluding applicable provincial and federal sales taxes.

Example:

A travel wholesaler purchased a block of seats from an air carrier.
Cost per ticket as purchased by travel wholesaler: $850.00
Cost of ticket sold by travel wholesaler to travel agent: $1,000.00
GST and PST on ticket sold to travel agent: $120.00
Total cost of ticket sold to travel agent:$1,120.00

The total gross sales reported for this transaction is $1,000.00, which is the cost of the sale to the travel agent excluding taxes. The sale is reportable regardless of whether the travel agency receives the payment directly or passes the payment information to another travel agency, travel wholesaler, or supplier.
