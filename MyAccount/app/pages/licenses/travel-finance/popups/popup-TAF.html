The Travel Assurance Fund contribution (TAF) contributioin

The Travel Assurance Fund contribution is 0.05% of your total gross sales. It is calculated by adding the gross sales as a travel agency and as a wholesaler (if applicable) and dividing by 2000. The minimum contribution is $100, even if the calculated contribution is less than $100.

Total gross sales TAF Amount
$0 to $200,000 $100
$2,000,000 $1,000
$10,000,000 $5,000
