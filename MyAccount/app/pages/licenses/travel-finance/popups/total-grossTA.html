Total gross sales is the total amount of travel services sold by a travel agency, in BC, during the most recent fiscal year. These services are the transaction value, or the cost of the sales to consumers, excluding applicable provincial and federal sales taxes.

Example:

A travel agency sells an airline ticket to a consumer.
Cost paid by travel agency for airline ticket: $900.00
Commission charged to consumer: $100.00
Base cost charged to consumer: $1,000.00
GST and PST charged to consumer: $120.00
Total cost charged to consumer: $1,120.00

The total gross sales reported for this transaction is $1,000.00, which is the cost of the sale to the consumer, excluding taxes. The sale is reportable regardless of whether the travel agency receives the payment directly or passes the payment information to another travel agent, travel wholesaler, or supplier.
