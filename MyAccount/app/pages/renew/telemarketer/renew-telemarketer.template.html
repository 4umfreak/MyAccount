Employees: Budgeted number of FTE's engaged in distance sales or 3rd party fundraising from this location calling from or calling into British Columbia. Use whole numbers only.

A Full Time Equivalent (FTE) is an employee representing 1957 hours for the license year October to September. FTE amounts are rounded to nearest whole number.

Example: 12.5 FTE = 13, 2.4 FTE = 2.
